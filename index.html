A tiny operating system for your attention.

TimePact is built around a simple constraint: during a pact, you may only use three apps or websites. Everything else becomes friction. That constraint becomes a promise – with yourself.

  1. Pick your 3 apps. The ones that genuinely move your work forward.
  2. Set a duration. 25 minutes, 90 minutes, or a custom block – whatever fits your brain.
  3. Honor the pact. Light‑touch check‑ins, self‑evaluation, and a worklog so you can see what actually happened.
  4. Review the data. Over time, TimePact will surface patterns in your focus, energy, and distraction triggers.

Built for the battle against drift.

We’re designing this for people who feel constantly pulled away – whether by AI tools, social feeds, or just too many open tabs. The goal is not guilt; it’s gentle accountability.

  • Friendly to ADHD and attention challenges.
  • Respects that you’ll sometimes break the rules – and asks why.
  • Optimized for clarity over complexity.
